What do we do

The Hero Association manages all of the cities heroes. The Hero Association uses a hierarchical class system to organize its heroes, with four classes and individually numbered ranks within respective classes (classes C, B, A, and S).

The Hero Association stands as a bastion of hope and valor in a world besieged by chaos and villainy. Founded in response to the escalating threats posed by superpowered individuals, it serves as a beacon of protection for ordinary citizens. Established by visionary leaders and bolstered by the courageous acts of countless heroes, the association embodies the ethos of altruism and duty. At its core, the Hero Association is a network of individuals united by a singular purpose: to safeguard humanity from the perils that lurk in the shadows. Its members hail from diverse backgrounds, possessing a wide array of powers and abilities. From super strength to telekinesis, each hero brings their unique talents to the forefront in the defense of peace and justice. The organization operates with meticulous efficiency, utilizing advanced technology and strategic coordination to combat threats swiftly and decisively. Central to its operations is the Hero Headquarters, a sprawling complex equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and command centers. Here, heroes gather to receive missions, train, and collaborate with one another in their tireless pursuit of safeguarding the innocent. But beyond its operational prowess, the Hero Association embodies a set of values that transcend mere heroics. Integrity, compassion, and selflessness form the bedrock of its ethos, guiding every decision and action undertaken by its members. Whether facing down malevolent supervillains or aiding communities ravaged by disaster, heroes of the association remain steadfast in their commitment to serving humanity. Yet, the path of a hero is not without its challenges. Faced with ever-evolving threats and moral dilemmas, members of the Hero Association must navigate a complex landscape fraught with danger and uncertainty. But it is through their unwavering resolve and indomitable spirit that they continue to stand as paragons of hope in a world fraught with darkness. In the annals of history, the Hero Association will be remembered not only for its valorous deeds but also for the ideals it represents. For as long as there are those who dare to stand against injustice, the beacon of the Hero Association will continue to shine brightly, illuminating the path towards a better tomorrow.
